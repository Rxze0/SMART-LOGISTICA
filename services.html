How we work

We've built a simple and straightforward delivery process that allows you to quickly and securely ship parcels throughout Kazakhstan. Every step is overseen by our team.

1

Reception of shipment

We receive the parcel, check its condition, record the details, and prepare it for delivery.

2

Sorting and processing

The shipment is sorted at the warehouse and sent along the optimal delivery route.

3

Shipping

The parcel is transported by our transport in compliance with deadlines and safety conditions.

4

Delivery to the recipient

The courier delivers the parcel personally to the recipient at the exact specified time.

Control at every stage

We use modern control systems to ensure every delivery is completed accurately and on time. Responsibility for your order is our guiding principle.
